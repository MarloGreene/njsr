# VA Disability Claim Guide (ZONK.VET)

A no-nonsense guide for veterans filing VA disability claims.

## Purpose

This guide helps OEF/OIF veterans understand and navigate the VA disability claims process with practical, actionable steps.

## Content Sections

### Introduction
- Who this guide is for
- The veteran perspective
- Why you deserve benefits

### Quick Facts
- Cost to file: $0
- Documents needed: DD-214
- Processing time: 90-180 days
- Potential rating: 10-100%

### Filing Steps
1. **Get Logged In**: VA.gov account setup (ID.me, DS Logon, MyHealtheVet)
2. **Get Your DD-214**: Request from vetrecs.archives.gov
3. **File Intent to File**: Establish your effective date
4. **Gather Evidence**: Medical records, buddy letters, service records
5. **Complete VA Form 21-526EZ**: The main disability claim form
6. **Attend C&P Exams**: Compensation and Pension examinations
7. **Wait and Track**: Monitor claim status

### Pro Tips
- Practical advice throughout each step
- Common mistakes to avoid
- Insider knowledge for success
